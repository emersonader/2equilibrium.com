Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

GramerTech LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates the 2Equilibrium wellness and weight loss mobile application (the "Service"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Email Address: Used for account creation, authentication, and important service communications
  • Full Name: Optional profile information for personalization

Health and Wellness Data

  • Weight Measurements: Weight logs you manually enter for progress tracking
  • Health Metrics: Data synced from Apple Health or Google Health Connect (steps, sleep, heart rate, calories)
  • Journal Entries: Your personal reflections and responses to daily lesson prompts
  • Progress Data: Lesson completion, quiz scores, badges earned, and streak information
  • Nutrition Logs: Food items and nutritional information you track

Usage Information

  • App Usage: Which lessons you complete, when you use the app, and how you interact with features
  • Device Information: Operating system version, app version, and basic device identifiers for technical support

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:

  • Provide personalized wellness content and recommendations
  • Track your progress through the 180-day program
  • Generate health insights and milestone celebrations
  • Sync your data across devices
  • Provide customer support
  • Process subscription payments
  • Send important service notifications (with your consent)
  • Improve our app features and content

Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored using Supabase, a secure cloud database service with industry-standard encryption. All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS protocols.

We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information.

Payment Processing

Subscription payments are processed by Stripe, a secure payment processor. We do not store your credit card information. For in-app purchases on iOS, payments are processed by Apple through the App Store.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Service Providers: Trusted services like Supabase (database), Stripe (payments), and Apple (in-app purchases) that help us operate the app
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our legal rights
  • Business Transfer: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with notice to you

All service providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the purposes we specify.

Your Data Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information in your profile
  • Deletion: Delete your account and all associated data through the app's settings
  • Portability: Export your journal entries and progress data
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from notifications at any time

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. After account deletion, we permanently remove all personal data within 30 days, except as required by law.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to know what personal information we collect and how it's used, and the right to delete your personal information.
